Sam

Bio

Sam loves people. Like, a lot. Spend ten minutes with the guy and tell us he doesn’t grow on you. Unassuming, encouraging, and fun, Sam also happens to love hair, and the machinations thereof. We think those two ingredients make him one of the best hairstylists in Cincinnati. Turns out we’re not alone: Cincinnati magazine’s “best stylist”? Yup. Sam Hills. Sam was formally trained at Michaels College of Hair Design, and later completed his apprenticeship at Tanya’s Image and Wellness Salon.

Playlist

Dr. Dog, Alt-J, Zeppelin, J Roddy Waltson and the Business, Rent to Own, The Mural Project and anything by Aaron Morgan

Trivia

He has an amazing wife and three beautiful kids. He loves fly fishing, basically anything caramel, and he used to be a professional musician.
