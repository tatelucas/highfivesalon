High Five’s hair product philosophy is pretty simple: We like things that do what they say they’re going to do. That thinking is what has led us to the products that are on our shelves. We don’t see a need to fill up our salon (or your bathroom cabinet) with every product out there; we simply want you to be using the right products for your hair. After trying and using almost every product line on the market, we’ve landed on what we think are the best quality products, made responsibly, with consistently excellent results. We’ve ended up with a small collection of the best hair care in the industry. We love this stuff and think you will too.

Caviar by Alterna:

The term “you get what you pay for” definitely applies when referring to the Caviar line from Alterna: These top shelf products boast some of the richest and most luxurious ingredients in hair care: From their moisture rich shampoos and conditioners to the touchable Working Hair Spray, Caviar has you covered. We’ve taken the time to hand pick our favorite products from the Caviar line up to keep things simple for you.
Caviar by Alterna Website

Kevin Murphy:

We think Kevin Murphy is a hair product genius. Kevin realizes you have to build great style from the bottom up, and offers fantastic shampoos and conditioners for any hair type. With that foundation, we can move on to health and volume: Kevin Murphy’s wide array of moisturizing lotions create either soft, sleek hair or long lasting body. Lastly, their finishing products (which go on dry hair- we love that!) enhance texture, shine or hold. We really dig the Kevin Murphy line of hair products, all the way around.
Kevin Murphy Website

Morrocan Oil:

This revolutionary product line has been taking over hair salons everywhere and at High Five we’ve opened the door and welcomed it right in. All of Moroccan Oil’s products contain a rich and healing base of Argan oil, derived from a nut harvested in the Middle East (probably, you know, in Morocco). With the consistency of an olive oil, Argan oil helps condition, smooth, and protect hair without compromising body and volume. So frizzy, damaged, or unruly hair beware, Moroccan Oil is your worst nightmare. Come try it in our store!
Morrocan Oil Website

American Crew:

This line of men’s hair care products makes any grooming problem a cinch. Working from a “puck” container format (the hockey puck-shaped screw-top containers with the goop inside), Crew offers a styling aid for any hair type and any style. American Crew offers great pomades, hair creams, and matte finish products that give you a ton of texture and control. Crew also understands that just because dudes want great hair doesn’t mean they want to smell like a woman. With classic smells like mint, citrus and rum, you’ll have no problem looking like a well-groomed man and smelling like one too. So come in and check ‘em out- we have a American Crew product that can make your hair looks its best.
American Crew Website
